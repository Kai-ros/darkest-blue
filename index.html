      GAME RULES:
    • The player will be dealt 5 cards to start.
    • The payer must play one card per turn.
    • The player will have a card automatically drawn from the top of their deck at the end of their turn to replace the card they just used.
    • Deck is comprised of 46 cards, split between 23 attack and heal cards.
    • Both player and opponent will have a health pool of 15 points. The first to reach 0 health points loses.
    • Have fun!!!
